Clive's fish

2 basa or sole (8 inch piece/ .5 in thick)
tbl sp olive oil
1/2 tsp cumin
1/2 tsp paprika
1/2 tsp garam masala / or Madras curry / or experiment
1/4 tsp salt

stir oil & spices together in small dish (should be runny paste)
place fish on tin foil
baste with paste (top only)
broil 8 min at 500 F
serve with brown rice and veg
